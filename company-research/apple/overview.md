Apple Overview:


## What Does Apple Do?
Apple is a multinational technology company that provides goods and services spanning from computers, smartphones, and software, with multi-level integration of software and hardware across products. The company also provides a marketplace ecosystem for application developers throughout the apple ecosystem.

## Main Products

Apple provides a variety of hardware products, including: Macbooks, iPhones, iPads, Wearables (Airpods, iWatch, Headphones, etc), Home and Accessories (Alexa (all variants),etc).

## Main Services

Apple's main services consist of software services, that include the App Store, and numerous other features such as Apple Music, and other software.

## Customers

The company is a multinational corporation selling all over the world. A large fraction of customers consist of individuals but also consist of corporations, with the largest share of customer sales in Q1 and Q2 of this year (and historically) coming from the Americas. 

## Revenue Sources

As reported in the 10-Q for Q2 of 2026, the most current data shows that the iPhone accounts for roughly half of all of Apple's revenue in Q2, and actually surpasses 60% if one factors in Q1 with Q2. Total Q2 revenue reaches $111 billion, with the iPhone accounting for roughly $57 billion dollars, followed by Services which reached net revenue of $31 billion. This is then followed in smaller proportion by Mac at $8 billion. Then Wearables, Home and Accessories with $7.9 billion, and the iPad with $6.9 billion. 

## Geographic Exposure

Apple primarily operates in 5 regions, defined as: Americas, Europe, Greater China, Japan, Resot of Asia Pacific. Note that Africa is not reported on whatsoever in the 10-Q. Net sales increased in all of these regions in Q2 of 2026, with the iPhone driving growth in all regions.
